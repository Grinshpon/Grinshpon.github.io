I wanted to write a blog so here it is. Consider this post the maiden post. The name is placeholder, or it might stay, idk. It means True/False in Russian. I came up with it as a reference to the Pravda newspaper, which was communist propaganda. This blog has nothing to do with commie stuff because I'm not a 12 year old anymore, or a liberal arts student, but I thought it'd be funny to name a blog something like that because I actually am 12.

I will use this blog to talk about mainly programming stuff, but also I may write about my hobbies, like video games and procrastinating. I will also try my hand at comedy. Avoid that. You can stop reading here.
